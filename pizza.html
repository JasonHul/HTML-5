Pizza

Invented in Italy by LIL CESAR. One of Italys most famous dish! Even though Americans eat the most of it.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Roll out your pizza dough in a cirle shape
  2. Spread the tomato sauce to cover the surface of the dough
  3. Add your cheese on top to cover the tomato sauce
  4. Add your pepperoni as toppings
  5. Cook for 15 mintues at 475 F
