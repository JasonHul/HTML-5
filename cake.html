Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake is a rich and moist dessert made with cocoa powder or melted chocolate, flour, sugar, eggs, and butter, typically layered and topped with chocolate frosting or ganache.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Coco Powder
  • Chocolate Frosting

Steps

  1. Mix the milk, eggs, sugar, flour, and coco poswder in a mixer
  2. Slowly add in your vanilla
  3. After mixed, pour cake batter into a cake pan
  4. Bake for 30 minutes at 350F
  5. When done baking, let cool
  6. Apply your chocolate frosting to cover the surfac of the cake
  7. Enjoy!
