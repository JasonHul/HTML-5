Lasagna

Lasagna is a dish made of pasta and sauces. It is also known as Garfields favorite food.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Noodles
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef and tomato sauce
  2. Layer the beef sauce, noodles and cheese
  3. Add your cheese on top
  4. Add your basil
  5. Cook for 25 mintues at 375 F
